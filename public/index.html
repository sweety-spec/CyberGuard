About CyberGuard

CyberGuard is designed to act as a digital bridge between citizens and cyber-crime resolution authorities. It provides a centralized platform where victims of online fraud, hacking, identity theft, and digital abuse can file complaints, receive tracking IDs, and monitor investigation progress in real time.

The system ensures transparency, accountability, and secure data handling, making the reporting and follow-up process far more reliable than scattered emails, phone calls, or manual tracking.
